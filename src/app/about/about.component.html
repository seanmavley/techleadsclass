MenPha (pronounce Men-Fa, meaning "Don't Take" in Twi) is crazily fast, and runs on all major platforms. You could even use it as a Mobile App on your Phone.

However, since that's not why you're here, know this. MenPha is a community driven platform. Before you buy your next second-hand electronic gadget, such as a phone, you could search to see if the device isn't a stolen or missing item.

Searching on MenPha is free. No sign up or sign in also required.

Enlist all your electronic gadgets as with you on the MenPha platform. Immediately you lose your item, simply mark it as stolen. The marked item will show up in searches thereafter.

Here's how to find the Unique IDs on your electronic gadgets.
The order of priority of what Unique ID to look for and add to MenPha is this:
IMEI > MEID > Serial Number > Any other ID

Phones (iP-roducts)

iPhone, iPad, etc, go into Settings > General > About. Dial, *#06# to bring up your IMEI.

Android Phones

For GSM phones, dial *#06#. In Android go to Settings > About

On Laptops

Different laptops have different ways to see Serial Number. Although sometimes found at the back of the device, it could be striped off. Research how to find the Serial Number of your laptop.
